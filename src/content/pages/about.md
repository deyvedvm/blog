Hey, I'm **Deyve Machado** — a software developer based in Brazil.

I work with backend and full-stack development, and I'm passionate about building things that are simple, reliable, and well-crafted. Over the years I've worked with Java, Kotlin, Node.js, and a handful of other technologies that kept things interesting.

## This blog

This is my space on the internet to write about what I learn, build, and think about as a developer. You'll find posts about software development practices, programming languages, tools I use, and lessons learned along the way.

I believe that writing is one of the best ways to solidify knowledge — so this blog is as much for me as it is for whoever stumbles upon it.
